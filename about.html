Our Story

In 1964, Capstone Toolstore was established to serve the local residents of Ramsbottom with common household tools. Its presence expanded over the decades, which led to the establishment of our annual catalogue in 1985. The company has become a household name within the Greater Manchester region, supplying local businesses and individual traders with specialist tools when needed.

Our unique range of products are popular amongst DIY enthusiasts due to their quality. We take pride in knowing that we only sell item that has been manufactured in this country.

We supply household name products as well as high-quality hand-made tools sourced from local manufacturer.

Our sales team are fully equipped to deal with your enquiry and will you help place your order, if you need special payment options please call our team and we will do everything in our power to help.
